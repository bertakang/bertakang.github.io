About Us

KB ENGINEERING Co., LTD is a trusted civil engineering firm based in Cambodia specializing in a wide range of high-quality engineering services. Founded in 2012 by a team of engineers with over 20 years of experience, we have been committed to delivering innovative solutions across various industries.

Our Services

    Mobile App and Web Design & Development

    We deliver seamless, custom-built digital solutions that enhance operational efficiency and user engagement for both static and dynamic mobile and web applications.

    Groundwater Surveying

    Using advanced techniques, we conduct detailed groundwater investigations and echo sounding surveys to assess water availability, quality, and underwater terrain. These services are critical for environmental sustainability, marine construction, and resource management.

    Land Surveying

    Our precise land surveying services offer critical data for project planning and development, providing accurate measurements for boundary and topographic assessments.

    Solar Design and Installation Services

    Our team offers reliable electrical system designs and precise load analysis to optimize performance and ensure safety in construction projects.

    Groundwater Well Design and Construction

    With extensive experience in water resources, we design and construct groundwater wells tailored to specific site conditions, ensuring efficient and sustainable water usage.

    Pump Design and Installation

    Our commitment to delivering high-quality pump design and installation services ensures sustainable, efficient, and reliable water management solutions.
