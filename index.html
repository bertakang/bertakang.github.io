Groundwater Surveying

KB Engineering utilizes advanced drilling equipment and techniques to conduct borehole drilling, collect soil and rock samples, perform geotechnical lab analysis, evaluate bearing geotechnical factors such as bearing capacity, foundation design, provides engineering recommendations and construction monitoring.

Groundwater Modeling and Analysis

KB Engineering utilizes computer models and simulation techniques to analyze the movement of groundwater and predict its behavior under various scenarios. This helps in understanding the flow patterns, identifying potential sources of contamination, and evaluating the impact of human activities on groundwater resources.

Well Design and Construction

KB Engineering can design and oversee the construction of wells and pumping systems to extract groundwater for various purposes, such as water supply, irrigation, and industrial uses.

Feasibility Studies

KB Engineering utilizes advanced drilling equipment and techniques to conduct borehole drilling, collect soil and rock samples, perform geotechnical lab analysis, evaluate bearing geotechnical factors such as bearing capacity, foundation design, provides engineering recommendations and construction monitoring.

Electrical System Design and Load Analysis

KB Engineering can design the electrical system of solar PV installation, including system sizing, the selection and configuration of PV modules, inverters, wiring, and interconnection with the grid or energy storage systems. KB Engineering can perform load analysis to assess the energy needs of the site and determine the appropriate size and capacity of the solar PV system to meet the demand.

Lightning Protection System Design

KB Engineering can implement lightning protection measures for solar PV systems that may include the following elements: lightning rods, down conductors, grounding system, surge protection devices and bonding.
